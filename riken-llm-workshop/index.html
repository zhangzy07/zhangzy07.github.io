RIKEN-AIP Internal Workshop on Large Language Models

Dates and Location Schedule Organizers
Co-organized by the Natural Language Understanding and the Imperfect Information Learning teams, this workshop aims to promote exchange between ML researchers interested in LLMs and LLM researchers seeking ML insights, with the goal of sparking new collaborations across teams. It will feature talks, group discussions, and brainstorming sessions on topics such as theory and algorithms, interpretability, open challenges, and impactful applications of ML in LLMs.

Dates and Location

  • Dates: October 2–3, 2025
  • Location: RIKEN-AIP Open Space, Tokyo + Online (with verification)
  • Accessibility: Open to all RIKEN-affiliated members
