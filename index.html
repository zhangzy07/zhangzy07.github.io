About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Imperfect Information Learning Team at RIKEN Center for Advanced Intelligence Project, led by Professor Masashi Sugiyama. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Nanjing University in 2022, where I was very fortunate to receive guidance from both Professor Yuan Jiang and Professor Zhi-Hua Zhou.

Research Interests

My research interests include topics in machine learning and data mining.
Most recently, I am interested in:

  • Weakly Supervised Learning
  • Machine Learning in Non-stationary Environments
